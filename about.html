Our mission & vision

We at Nordic Trekk aim to satisfy each and every one of our customers with a wide range of trekking products for each kind of trekking experience. Are you a beginner who wishes to venture into the world of trekking? Great! We've got the products. Are you an experienced trekker who wishes to trekk in harsh terrains on the biggest mountains? We've got you covered! At Nordic Trekk, we believe the customers should always get what they paid for, and our wide selection of high-quality trekking products prove that is the case with us!

Compliant with virtually every environmental-friendly marking, our products are made with up to 90 percent recycled material, and are sure to please even the most picky environmentalists! Trekking for us is more than just a hobby, its a lifestyle. This enables us to put 110 percent of our enthusiasm and energy into making the best boots, belts, backpacks, and accessories for your needs, sparing nothing to bring the quality up to the highest standard.

Our company

Since our grand opening on the 24th of september, our business has expanded rapidly. Once just an idea, Nordic Trekk has now become one of the biggest online shops for trekking. With around 4 employees working from home on their laptops, Nordic Trekk is powered by some of the greatest minds, known both inside and-out the trekking community. Our goal is to deliver the products you need for the ultimate trekking experience, and so far, we've had zero returns or complaints. How's that for a review?
